The Story

A Quiet Corner of the Sahyadris

Manu Villa is a peaceful homestay overlooking the beautiful Bhatgar Dam backwaters. Surrounded by more than 50 trees inside Sahyadri Farm, it is the perfect place for families, friends and nature lovers looking for a relaxing getaway.

  • ✔ Nestled on a private working farm in the Sahyadri foothills
  • ✔ Uninterrupted views of the Bhatgar backwaters
  • ✔ A calm, green retreat away from the city
